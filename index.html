Pebble HF Web Firmware Updater

Update your Pebble HF firmware right from your browser — no software to install.

How it works

  1. Pick a firmware version from the dropdown below — it downloads automatically. (Or choose Upload my own .hex file to use your own.)
  2. Plug your STK500v1 adapter into your computer, then connect the other end to your Pebble HF — making sure the pinout orientation is correct.
  3. Click "Connect & Flash" and pick your adapter from the browser's list.
  4. Wait for the Writing and Verifying bars to finish — that's it.
Web Serial is not available in this browser.
This tool needs the Web Serial API (navigator.serial), which is only available in Chromium-based browsers — Chrome, Edge, Brave, or Opera — served over https:// or localhost. Please switch browsers and reload.
